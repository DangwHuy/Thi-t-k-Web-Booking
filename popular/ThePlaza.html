VIP rooms

highest level of service

VIP rooms in hotels are specially designed to offer a premium, luxurious experience, typically catering to important guests or those seeking the highest level of service.VIP rooms are more spacious than regular rooms, often featuring separate living and sleeping areas, and sometimes a private workspace. VIP amenities include a king-size bed with high-quality linens, a large flat-screen TV, premium sound systems, and an espresso coffee maker. The bathroom is fully equipped, often featuring a jacuzzi or double bathtub, along with luxury toiletries and plush bathrobes. In addition, VIP guests typically enjoy special services such as a private reception, 24/7 room service, and access to exclusive areas like VIP lounges, conference rooms, or other premium hotel amenities. Some VIP rooms even offer a private balcony or terrace with exclusive views.
